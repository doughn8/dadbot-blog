About Dadbot⌗

Welcome to Dadbot - a blog covering everything from the latest tech trends to the trials and tribulations of parenting, with a healthy dose of sports, finance, and yes, the occasional deep dive into conspiracy theories.

What You’ll Find Here⌗

  • Technology - Reviews, tutorials, and thoughts on the latest in tech
  • Parenting - Real talk about the dad life
  • Crypto/Finance - Navigating the world of money and digital assets
  • Sports - Commentary and analysis
  • News - Takes on current events
  • Conspiracy Corner - Down the rabbit hole we go
  • Hobbies - Everything else that makes life interesting

About the Author⌗

Just a dad with too many browser tabs open and not enough time to read them all. This blog is my attempt to organize the chaos.

Automation Note⌗

This blog is designed to be automation-friendly using n8n workflows. Posts can be created programmatically, making it easy to maintain a consistent publishing schedule.
