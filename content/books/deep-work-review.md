## The Core Argument

Deep work is a competitive advantage. You get better results by working with intensity and fewer context switches.

### What Helped Most

- Scheduling focus blocks like meetings
- Reducing inbox checks to set times
- Tracking output over hours worked

### What I Changed

I moved all notifications off my phone and built two 90-minute focus blocks into my mornings.

### Rating: 4/5

Great if you need a reset. Some ideas repeat, but the habits are practical.
