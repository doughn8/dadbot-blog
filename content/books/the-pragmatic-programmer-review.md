## Why This One Sticks

The Pragmatic Programmer is a gentle push toward responsibility, curiosity, and continuous improvement.

### Standout Ideas

- Own your work end to end
- Build good tooling habits
- Automate anything repeated

### What I Applied

I started logging tiny process improvements weekly. After two months, my workflow felt smoother and faster.

### Rating: 5/5

If you only read one software book this year, make it this one.
