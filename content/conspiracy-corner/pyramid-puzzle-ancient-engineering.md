## Down the Rabbit Hole

Let me be clear: I'm not saying it was aliens. But let's look at some facts that are genuinely puzzling.

### The Engineering Problem

The Great Pyramid of Giza:
- 2.3 million stone blocks
- Average weight: 2.5 tons each
- Some blocks weigh 80+ tons
- Built in approximately 20 years

Do the math: that's placing one block every 2-3 minutes, 24/7, for two decades.

### What We Know

Modern engineers have proposed various theories:
1. Ramps (internal or external)
2. Lever systems
3. Water lubrication
4. Massive workforce organization

### What's Weird

- Precision alignment to true north (within 0.05 degrees)
- The ratio of the base perimeter to height equals 2π
- Similar structures on multiple continents with no known contact

### My Take

Ancient humans were incredibly intelligent and capable. We often underestimate our ancestors.

But here's the thing: even experts admit we can't fully replicate these structures with modern technology (mainly due to economics, not capability).

### The Real Conspiracy

Maybe the real mystery isn't *how* but *why*. What motivated entire civilizations to dedicate generations to these projects?
