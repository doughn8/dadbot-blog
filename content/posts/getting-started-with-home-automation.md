## The Smart Home Journey Begins

After years of resisting, I finally took the plunge into home automation. Here's what I learned so you don't have to make the same mistakes I did.

### Why Bother?

The appeal is obvious: lights that turn on when you walk in, thermostats that know your schedule, and the ability to check if you locked the door from anywhere. But is it worth the hassle?

**Spoiler alert:** Yes, but with caveats.

### Where to Start

1. **Pick an ecosystem** - Apple HomeKit, Google Home, or Amazon Alexa
2. **Start small** - Smart bulbs or plugs are the gateway drug
3. **Consider local control** - Home Assistant for the privacy-conscious

### The Dad Tax

Every smart home project takes 3x longer than expected. Factor this into your plans, especially if you have kids asking "is it done yet?" every 5 minutes.

### My Current Setup

- Philips Hue lights throughout
- Ecobee thermostat
- Home Assistant running on a Raspberry Pi
- Various Zigbee sensors
